What is Paria Theatre?
{% include icons/icon-down-arrow.html %}

Paria Theatre is a place for people to explore their ideas and creativity in a safe space. It’s a place to play and be a kid again. It’s a place where anyone is welcome.

The ambition of Paria Theatre is to be a hub of imagination and delight by creating theatre through improvisation. This means that we play with the hilariously funny and the heartbreakingly sad, often at the same time. We put community at the heart of what we do and take care to support everyone who joins us no matter what. We pride ourselves on giving everybody an opportunity.

Paria Theatre was born out of a love of grounded and realistic Improv, that took its time to make real and interesting characters.
It started out as Paria Exchange, a double act between founders Ben Macpherson and Gareth Hughes in 2015. They liked what they found so much that they wanted to get other people involved to play with what they had found so Paria Theatre was born. Paria Exchange is still one of the headline shows of Paria Theatre.
